1D to 5D - Food Contamination

New York, NY

Step#1 - 1D Poster


While getting inspired by the previous project - 7 in 7, I am defining my design directions for this project. The process includes finding 40 - 50 inspiration words, selecting 5 favorite words and studying a quote from relative artists/designers/engineers about each word.

Step#2 - Brainstorming for 2D/3D


Through the brainstorming, I am guided to define my design scope, research the precedents, and initiate design ideas, explore the design forms step by step.

Step#3 - 2D: Billboard


For this project, I select food contamination as my design domain and my design objective is to bring up public attention on the current issue through the design of billboard and poster and provide useful information through the design of wallet-size cards.


Related Information: https://www.cdcfoundation.org/pr/2015/CDC-protects-Americans-from-foodborne-illnesses
