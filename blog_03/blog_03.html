7 in 7 - Idea #3 - Time Lapse

New York, NY

Inspiration


Apple is a symbol that expressing different meanings to different individual or groups based on the cultural background, religion, personal experience. Thus, I chose apple as the design form because I think it’s a symbol that people can relate to and have different opinions. So this project would express different meanings to individuals.

Design Element & Form


Design idea is based on a sculpture project I worked on during my undergraduate study. It is a sculpture that transforming a bitted apple into a series of sculpture apples. The idea behind it is transforming a natural product into a large scale, into a series of four by using artificial materials: form and gesso. The series helps to add the time as a dimension into the design and showcase how an “apple” changed per each time. To be more literary, it could be read as a visualization of food waste problem or food safety problem.

Precedents


Present a series of blueberries made by artificial materials by using photoshop.

Present incremental balls and showcase a strong sense of the variation.
