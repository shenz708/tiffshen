We live in a time period that each of us receives a huge amount of information everyday and there are so many ways to save all of the information. It is a powerful tool for us, however, we always can refer back to it, we don't enjoy the moment we are having it. Design Element & Form


One design idea came into my mind is a ephemeral reading digital work, which means the context you are reading is disappearing sentence by sentence as you read through, so you have to focus on the reading because there is no paper copy or backup file.

My design intention is to bring my audience to know we only live in the present moment and enjoy the moment through exploring disparate visualization methods.

Precedents


Present each word on top of the previous words by using motion graphic.

Present literary works in a form of circle to blur the order of the context.
