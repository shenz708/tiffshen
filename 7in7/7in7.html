7 in 7 - Here and Now

New York, NY

Nowadays we live in a era with abundant information, resources, datas, and statistics. The overloading of information sometimes exceed our capability to understand the issues or make effective decisions. So we tend to save the data first and then pull it out when we need it, gradually we don’t enjoy the moment of having things as we used to. Here and Now is a series of design that aims to evoke and encourage people to live in the moment and appreciate the present.

Day#1 - Infinite


Description: “Infinite" is a looping poetry that only allow audiences to read single word at bottom. It creates an ephemeral moments of each words and evokes audiences to enjoy the moments of reading every words. 

Day#2 - Flowing

Description: “Flowing” is a moving text strings that  expresses the flowing of time and encourages audiences to focus on the "happening moment", through controlling the paces and scales of each words.  

Day#3 - A Fleeting Show

Description: “A Fleeting Show” is a poetry that presented in a transient format. As you reading from the first paragraph shows on left upper side to the last paragraph shows on right lower side, the paragraph is continuously disappearing so the reader need focus on the present moment of each paragraph.

Day#4 - The Sparkle

Description: “The Sparkle” is a poetic and artistic expression of memorable moments in our life. The curvy wave at the bottom presents the flowing of time. When the circles hit ground, it triggers single circle generate multiple circles, single color turn to multiple colors, it is mimicking the happening moment, where things are formatting/generating/growing, where your experiencing moment become your sparkling/gleaming moments in your timeline.

Day#5 - The Calendar

“The calendar” is a figurative expression of how I understand time. People use calendar to organize days and manage their schedules. We divided time into days, weeks, months and years, and put corresponding to-do list into each time slot. But life is not about counting days and chasing your schedules, because you only live in the current moment, is more about treasuring the current moment.

Day#6 - The Disappearing

Description: “The Disappearing” is a design that showing the format changing from substantial and existent to slight and immaterial. It conveys the idea of forgetting the fixed past that drag you down and embrace the present that you are experiencing.

Day#7 - The Forming

Description: “The Forming” is a design using particle as an element to express the idea of repetition of forming and collapsing of the current moment. The format changing from unordered to ordered, from chaotic to quiet, from turbulent to organized is intended to express how your present only exists right now and soon it would become your past.
