7 in 7 - Idea #1

New York, NY

Spatial organization is defined as organizing a series of spaces into one recognizable whole. With experience in interior design, I’ve been studying and working on projects aim to provide various functions for target audiences through smart space planning space smartly. The relationship of parts to the whole has been always inspired me to explore this topic. I want to explore this topic not just through the lens of interior designer, but also from a broader perspective by applying multidisciplinary approaches.

Influenced by Kuhio Beach, the boundaries between indoor and outdoor are blurred.
