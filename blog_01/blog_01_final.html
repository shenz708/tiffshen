Inspiration


Spatial organization is defined as organizing a series of spaces into one recognizable whole. Design Element & Form


I want to explore this design topic through using silhouette of each rooms as a design element. The intention is to using the element to impress how a whole is divided into parts and explore the relation between each parts.
